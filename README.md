# PulpAI - The Unified Intelligence Layer
"Communication is fragmented. Relationships shouldn't be."

Pulp is a Generative AI communication layer designed to eliminate friction in professional workflows. By transforming WhatsApp into a remote control for your digital life, we solve the "Email Overload" problem through a Human-in-the-Loop approach, ensuring AI efficiency without loss of control.

🔴 The Problem: The "Context Switch" Fatigue
Professionals waste 30% of their day on "Zeitfresser" (time-wasters): switching between Outlook, Slack, and LinkedIn to find information.

Fragmentation: Data is trapped in silos.

Inertia: Replying to a formal email while on the go is high-friction.

Trust Gap: Users don't trust autonomous agents to speak for them.

🗺️ Roadmap: Three Phases to Market Dominance
Phase 1: The "Zero-Inertia Inbox" (MVP - Current)
We bridge the gap between Gmail and WhatsApp using a sophisticated Orchestration Layer.

Style Mirroring: Our engine analyzes the recipient's tone (formal/casual) and drafts a perfect response.

Confidence through Control: No message is sent without a one-tap approval.

Impact: High-speed communication with zero friction.

Phase 2: The Pulp App (Generative UI Platform)
Transitioning from a bot to a native platform. We replace static text with Generative UI.

Dynamic Adaptation: The interface "invents" itself in real-time. If an email suggests a meeting, Pulp renders a calendar widget. If it's an invoice, a payment terminal appears.

Platform Mirroring: Every service (Gmail, Slack, Teams) gets its own dedicated stream within the Pulp ecosystem.

Tech: Vercel AI SDK, React Server Components.

Phase 3: Identity Aggregation (The Game Changer) 🏆
The ultimate evolution: Shifting from Channel-Centric to Person-Centric communication.

One Thread. Every Channel: All interactions with "Peter Lustig" live in one single chat.

Unified Intelligence: Peter's email from this morning, his Slack message from noon, and his LinkedIn DM from tonight appear in one chronological timeline.

Identity Mapping Engine: Pulp automatically links different handles (Email, Slack ID, Socials) to a single human entity.

✨ Key Value Propositions (USPs)
Human-in-the-Loop (HITL): We provide the speed of an agent with the safety of a human signature.

Zero-Inertia Workflow: Eliminate the barrier between receiving information and taking action.

AI Efficiency without Loss of Control: We automate the "Drecksarbeit" (grunt work) while leaving the final "Go" to the user.

🛠 Tech Stack (The Vision)
Frontend: Next.js (Tailwind CSS) + Vercel AI SDK for Generative UI.

Orchestrator: TypeScript/Node.js backend managing multi-channel Webhooks.

Memory: Vector Database (Pinecone) for cross-channel context.

Connectivity: Official APIs for Gmail, Slack, Telegram, and Meta Graph.
